Why Africa Matters

More than a continent — a world within a world.

Africa is the second-largest continent on Earth, covering 20% of the world's total land area. It is home to the world's oldest universities, the longest river, and the largest desert. Modern humanity originated here over 300,000 years ago.

From the snowcapped peaks of the Rwandan volcanoes to the ancient pyramids of Egypt, from the jazz-soaked streets of Cape Town to the towering skyscrapers of Lagos — Africa defies every stereotype.
